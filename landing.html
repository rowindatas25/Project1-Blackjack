Classic Blackjack

Instructions

  • The goal of the game is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21
  • Face Cards(Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 and Aces are worth 1 or 11 depending on the player's score
  • The player and dealer each start with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end
  • To Hit is to ask for another card, to Stay is to hold your total and end your turn.
  • If you go over 21 at any point, the dealer wins no matter what the dealer has
  • If you are dealt 21 with your first two cards (Ace, 10), you got a Blackjack
  • Blackjack means you earn 1.5 the amount of the bet you made
  • The dealer will hit until their cards are 17 or higher
  • To Double is like a Hit but the bet is doubled and you only receive one more card
